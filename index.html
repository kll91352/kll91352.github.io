About Miracle

I am apart of the Ignite Committee in Miracle and the purpose of this organization is to help raise money for the children's hospital. This is very important to me because I think it is crucial to support Children's Healthcare of Atlanta and help out the kids who are extremely sick. I was first exposed to dance marathon at my High School in Charleston, South Carolina. We called it Wandothon because I went to Wando High School. Dance Marathon is a 24 hour event full with activities and raising money. However, Wandothon was only 7 hours during the day but we still raised a lot of money!

There are many Committee's in Miracle but the one with only freshman is Ignite, which is the one I am apart of. We meet every other Wednesday and talk about fundraising events that are coming up before dance marathon. In Ignite we have small events two months leading up to the event where we celebrate all of the money we have fundraised for the hospital.

Some events and activities that we have already participated in to raise money is 100 Dollar Day and Ignite Olympics. 100 Dollar Day is when you have to raise 100 dollars in 24 hours. There are also color groups in Ignite so which ever color group raises the most amount of money gets a prize.

Benefits of The Organization

  1. Raises money for a Children's Healthcare of Atlanta
  2. Communicates with families who are struggling
  3. Organizes events to raise the most amount of money
  4. Creates a close knit community
